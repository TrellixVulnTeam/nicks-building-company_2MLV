Our History

Nick's Building Company has been building, repair, and renovating houses, offices, restaurants, and other projects for 30 plus years. Established in 1960, we took an oath to provide our customers with the most modern projects they could have at affordable prices, we believe everybody has the right to own a property in this country. Ever since Nick's Building Company have had from customers from all over the country, we strictly follow the most up to date guidelines when executing our projects, we're very easy to communicate to and we finished our projects within the timeframe that we give our customers. Today, we still have thousands of clients we're working for so don't hesitate to give us a call.
